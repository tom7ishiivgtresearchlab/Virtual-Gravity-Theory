## Overview

This folder contains **VGT Part II**, which presents Phases 4–11 of the Virtual Gravity Theory (VGT) framework:

- **Theoretical consistency** (Phases 4–7): Scale-dependent coupling formalism, vacuum interaction, early-universe consistency
- **Observational reconstruction** (Phases 8–9): Field reconstruction, joint constraints from cosmic chronometers + BAO + SN Ia
- **Stability and predictions** (Phases 10–11): Linearized perturbations, consistency checks, falsifiable predictions for Euclid/DESI/JWST

VGT II extends the empirical foundation established in Part I (Phases 1–3) by developing the theoretical underpinnings and providing quantitative predictions for upcoming observational surveys (2025–2030).

## Key Results

- **Best-fit parameters** (57 data points):
  - H₀ = 69.4 ± 0.7 km/s/Mpc
  - Ωₘ = 0.307 ± 0.012
  - α₀ = (3.8 ± 0.9) × 10⁻⁵
  - χ²_red = 1.08

- **Consistency checks**:
  - |ΔG/G| < 6.5 × 10⁻⁵ over z ≲ 2.3
  - Compatible with LLR/binary pulsar constraints
  - CMB acoustic peak shift < 0.1%

- **Falsifiable predictions**:
  - σ₈(z=1) = 0.78 ± 0.03 (Euclid weak lensing)
  - H(z=2) = 225 ± 8 km/s/Mpc (DESI BAO)
  - +1.5σ high-z structure excess vs. ΛCDM (JWST)
