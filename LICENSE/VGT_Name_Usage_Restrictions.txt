Virtual Gravity Theory (VGT) — Name Usage Restrictions

This document defines additional name-protection rules 
governing the use of the term “Virtual Gravity Theory (VGT)” 
and all derived terminology.

1. The scientific term “Virtual Gravity Theory (VGT)” may be used  
   freely for academic discussion, citation, non-commercial education,  
   and scientific critique.

2. The following uses require explicit written permission  
   from the author (Tsutomu Ishii):
   - Commercial use of the name “VGT” or “Virtual Gravity Theory”.
   - Branding, product naming, or service naming that includes “VGT”.
   - Use of “VGT” in a manner implying endorsement or collaboration.
   - Creation of derivative theories that include the name “VGT”.

3. The name must not be used for:
   - Misleading scientific claims
   - Fraudulent activities
   - Political, financial, religious, or ideological manipulation

4. When referencing VGT in legitimate academic contexts,  
   proper attribution must be provided:
   Ishii, Tsutomu — Virtual Gravity Theory (VGT), 2025.

These restrictions do not limit the freedoms granted  
under the CC BY 4.0 license for the *content itself*.  
They apply only to protection of the “VGT” name and identity.
